A collection of Claude artifacts I built to help with language learning.

## Quickstart

To use these tools, you need a free Claude account:

1. Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and sign up for a free account
2. Click on any tool below to open it in Claude
3. Start using the artifact directly in your browser

## Tools

### [Reading Helper](read-help)

An interactive tool to assist with reading comprehension in foreign languages, with built-in flashcard support.

### [Translation Validator](trans-check)

A tool to validate and correct your translations with explanations for mistakes.
